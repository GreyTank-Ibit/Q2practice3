Vinland Saga


Synopsis: Vinland Saga's timeline is around the time of Vikings and their then peak in the North Sea and England. The First season is action packed and centered around Thorfinn the MC, while Season 2 is more on Redemption and the aftermath of it all.


You have no enemies. No one has enemies. There is no reason to harm anyone in the world.
Character Name Description Appearance
Thorfinn The Main Character, Thorfinn goes through the Saga starting as a kidnapped child to a then grown up warrior seeking redemption.
Askeladd A secondary Main Character, Askeladd is the one who teaches Thorfinn the most of what he used to become a warrior. Askeladd is also the one who killed Thorfinn's Father.
Prince Canute Another secondary Main Character, Prince Canute goes from a scared and timid prince into a ruthless king by the end of season 2, being the then enemy of Thorfinn as compared to ally during season 1.
